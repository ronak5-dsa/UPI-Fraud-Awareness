QR code scams

Fraudsters send fake QR codes asking for payments. Scanning may drain your account!

Phishing calls

Fake calls pretending to be bank/customer care asking for OTP or UPI PIN.

SIM swap

Scammer gets a new SIM issued for your number and takes control of your bank-linked OTPs.
